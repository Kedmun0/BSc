3rd February 2020

Club Shake Up

Following the Annual General Meeting held on the 3rd February 2020 Lossiemouth FC have announced that Alan McIntosh has been voted in as the new club Chairman. Kevan McIntosh will take the reins of the Secretary role, while James Allan will continue to operate as the Club Treasurer.The club is now looking forward to finishing the season on a high, before making further progress on and off the field throughout the summer and into the 2020-21 season. Lossiemouth FC (16th) are back in action this Saturday when they welcome Clachnacuddin (15th) to Grant Park. Manager Joe Russell added: “This is a massive game for the lads. We can get equal on points with Clach with a win so this is a real six-pointer.”
