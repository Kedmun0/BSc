Lossiemouth FC Child Wellbeing and Protection Policies

Child Protection Officer

Lossiemouth Football Club has appointed the following persons to the post of Child Protection Officer and assistant. As you will see both have passed all relevant courses required for the roles.

  1. Laura Williams (mobile: 07817642086): LFC Child Protection and Wellbeing Officer.
  2. Lisa Dow: LFC Deputy Child Protection and Wellbeing Officer.

Laura has been a teacher for 36 years; she initially completed a Safeguarding and Child Protection Workshop. As that expired after three years, she completed the on-line CWSF on 010519. As mandated by the SFA, she has completed the Children’s wellbeing and protection officers’ course and attends all mandated SFA Child Wellbeing meetings. She also has a signed Terms of Reference, also an SFA requirement. Lisa has completed the same courses as Laura.
